Data description:
Internet Advertisements dataset represents a set of possible advertisements on
the internet. The dataset is 20 years old, donated July 1st, 1998 to the UCI ML
Repository. It contains 3279 instances and 1558 attributes. Three of the attributes
are continuous while all other attributes are binary.

Binary questions:
Is image height a predictor of advertisement?
Do caption terms predict an advertisement?

Non-binary questions:
Which attributes are the best predictors of an advertisement?
Of the advertisements, how many have caption terms?

Future improvements to data set:
Image color
Image position on webpage
Text on webpage where image resides

Source: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Internet+Advertisements
